About Us

Who we are

Seaside.co is a summer clothing brand that was founded by two friends who had a passion for the beach and a desire to create a brand that embodied the spirit of summer. What began as a small online store selling a few items of clothing has now grown into one of the most popular summer clothing brands in the country.

Our mission

At Seaside.co, our mission is to provide our customers with stylish and functional clothing that is perfect for the beach and other summer activities. We prioritize quality and customer satisfaction, and always seek to incorporate new and innovative designs into our product line. We believe that summer is a time for fun, relaxation, and making memories, and we strive to create clothing that embodies that carefree and relaxed spirit. Our ultimate goal is to become the go-to brand for summer clothing and accessories, and to help people around the world look and feel their best during the hottest months of the year.
